Who I Am

I'm Chen See Ng (黄靖思), a frontend developer and digital designer currently completing my MSc in Interactive Digital Media at Griffith College Dublin. I'm based in Dundalk and looking for roles in Dublin or remote.

What sets me apart is that I sit at the intersection of both design and engineering. I don't just build what a designer hands me, and I don't just design without thinking about how it gets built. I do both — and I think that combination is rare and valuable.

I care deeply about the details that most people skip: the curve on a hover transition, the rhythm of a type scale, the way a page loads. I believe the best interfaces are the ones you don't consciously notice — only what they make possible.

I'm actively looking for an internship or junior frontend / UI role where I can contribute from day one and keep growing fast.

How I Work

My process

01
Understand
I start by understanding who this is for, what it needs to communicate, and what success looks like — before touching any tool.
02
Design First
Typography, layout, colour, motion — I think through the visual language before writing a line of code. Figma is my sketchpad.
03
Build Clean
Semantic HTML, maintainable CSS, readable JS. I write code that a future developer — or future me — will actually enjoy reading.
04
Refine
Test on real devices, tweak until every pixel is right. The difference between good and great is always in the details.
