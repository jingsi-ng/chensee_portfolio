Who I Am

I'm Chen See Ng (黄靖思). I am currently pursuing my MSc in Interactive Digital Media at Griffith College Dublin. I'm based in Dundalk and actively looking for roles in Dundalk, Dublin or remote working opportunites.

Before moving into web development, I spent several years working as a teacher. This background taught me how to present complex information clearly and always think from the learner's or user's perspective. Today, I apply the same "people-first" mindset to web design. I enjoy the entire process of designing user-friendly interfaces in Figma and then bringing them to life with clean code.

For me, good design is about solving problems and making things easy to use. I enjoy fine-tuning the small details, whether it is adjusting a layout for mobile screens or ensuring a website is accessible to everyone. I believe a great website should feel smooth and intuitive to navigate without confusing the user.

I'm currently looking for a junior web design or frontend role where I can continue to learn, grow and create meaningful digital experiences alongside a great team.

How I Work

My process

01
Understand
I always start by researching the target audience and the main goal of the project. It is important to understand what the user truly needs before opening any design software
02
Design First
I plan the layout, colours and typography carefully. I usually use Figma to create wireframes and visual prototypes so that I have a clear direction before I begin coding.
03
Build Clean
I focus on writing semantic HTML, organised CSS and clear JavaScript. My goal is to build responsive websites that are easy to maintain and accessible for all users.
04
Refine
Finally, I test the website on different devices and screen sizes. I always double-check the small details to make sure the final product works smoothly and looks great everywhere.
